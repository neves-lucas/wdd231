Habit Tracker Site Plan

Lucas Rocha

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to demonstrate what I have learned so far in this course.

Audience

My audience will be anyone wanting to develop good habits and get rid of bad ones.

Site Name

"HabitOk"

This name represents being chill with the good habits that you have developed and knowing that it is "Ok" to have bad habits, as long as you are working to get rid of them.

Site purpose

The site will provide a place where people can get comprehensive information about why habits are so important as well as how to develop them.

Scenarios

  • Why are habits so important?
  • How can I develop good habits?
  • How can I get rid of bad habits?
  • What are some of the good habits and how they affect my life?
